Welcome to Tochis Bistro

Tochi's Bistro is a grand and bustling Parisian brasserie.

Located in the heart of Lagos, Tochi's Bistro is a grand and bustling Parisian brasserie. With a relaxed and lively atmosphere, it is perhaps best known for serving traditional French food at remarkably good value. In addition, the building also plays host to the ZL Café, a casual pavement-level café.
